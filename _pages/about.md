# About Me

Hello, readers! My name is Sitong, you could also call me steamer, and I am thrilled to have you here on my blog. Allow me to introduce myself and share a glimpse of my professional journey.

# Education

I obtained my bachelor’s degree in software engineering from the prestigious Beijing Institute of Technology in 2017. This educational foundation has equipped me with a solid understanding of software engineering principles and a strong technical background.

# Work Experience

Following my graduation, I embarked on a rewarding professional journey in the field of IT. From September 2017 to June 2021, I served as a *department manager* in the IT Department of Software at the **National Computer Science Center**. During my tenure, I had the privilege of leading a team and overseeing various software projects, honing my leadership and project management skills.

In August 2021, I joined **Pony.ai**, a renowned company in the autonomous driving industry, as a *DevOps Engineer* in the Infrastructure Department. Over the course of my employment until May 2023, I actively contributed to the development and maintenance of their complex infrastructure. This experience allowed me to work with cutting-edge technologies and further expand my expertise on IaC.

# Technical Expertise

In my daily work, I primarily utilize Python, Shell, and Go programming languages. These languages serve as the backbone of my work, enabling me to tackle diverse challenges efficiently. Additionally, I possess advanced knowledge and extensive experience in operating systems and network architecture. This expertise has proven invaluable in successfully handling complex infrastructure within large platforms at prominent organizations.

# Join Me on this Journey

Through this blog, I aim to share my insights, experiences, and knowledge with you. Together, we will explore various topics related to software engineering, DevOps, and network architecture. I hope to inspire and provide practical guidance to fellow professionals, aspiring engineers, and technology enthusiasts.

Thank you for joining me on this exciting adventure. And feel free to give out your comment under my articles. Let’s learn and grow together!
